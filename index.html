Frequently asked questions

How do I track my UDAs?
Download UDA Pulse for free on iOS or Android. Set your annual UDA target and contract details, then log each treatment as you complete it. The app calculates your UDA delivery in real time with a progress ring showing where you stand against your target.
What is a UDA?
A Unit of Dental Activity (UDA) is the measure used by the NHS to value dental treatments in England. Band 1 treatments generate 1 UDA, Band 2 generates 3 UDAs (with Band 2a/2b/2c sub-bands introduced from 25 November 2022), and Band 3 generates 12 UDAs. NHS dental associates are typically contracted to deliver a set number of UDAs per year. See the LDC explainer at ldc.org.uk/what-is-a-uda and the NHSBSA Band 2 guidance at nhsbsa.nhs.uk for the official definitions, and the BDA UDA Value Checker at bda.org for value benchmarks.
Is there an app for tracking dental UDAs?
Yes. UDA Pulse is a free mobile app built specifically for NHS dentists to track UDA delivery. It supports NHS associates, locum dentists, salaried dentists, and mixed/private practitioners. Available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).
Does UDA Pulse work offline?
Yes. UDA Pulse stores all data locally on your device with no account or internet connection required. Your data never leaves your phone. You can export reports as CSV or PDF at any time.

Can I use it for private and NHS work?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device — nothing is sent to the cloud. No account is required and no patient data is collected. Yes. UDA Pulse has built-in voice entry that works offline. Say "Band 2" or "three Band 1s" to log NHS treatments hands-free. Basic UDA voice commands are free for all users. Premium unlocks voice for private entries and batch commands like "two Band 2s and a crown for 450".
Does UDA Pulse have home screen widgets?
Yes. UDA Pulse offers home screen widgets for both iOS and Android in 4 sizes. Choose between Today, This Month, or This Year views. Widgets show your UDA progress ring, earnings, and pace status. Colours are fully customisable. Tap any widget to open the app.
How does UDA Pulse calculate tax for dentists?
UDA Pulse calculates UK income tax, National Insurance Class 2 and 4, and student loan repayments from your logged NHS and private income. It supports sole trader, limited company (with corporation tax and dividends), and PAYE entity types per practice. The personal allowance taper is correctly applied above £100k. VAT threshold monitoring alerts you when approaching the £90k registration threshold.
Can I import data from Dentally or EXACT?
Yes. UDA Pulse can import CSV exports from Dentally, EXACT (Software of Excellence), and SFD (Software for Dentists). The parser auto-detects the format from column headers and maps Band, Date, Patient, and Amount fields automatically. This eliminates the need to re-enter data that already exists in your practice management system.
What are the 2026/27 UDA band values?
Under the April 2026 NHS dental contract: Band 1 = 1 UDA (exam, scale & polish, prevention), Band 2a = 3 UDA (1-2 fillings, simple extractions), Band 2b = 5 UDA (multiple fillings, anterior endo), Band 2c = 7 UDA (molar endo, surgical extractions), Band 3 = 12 UDA (crowns, bridges, dentures), Urgent = flat fee. Fissure sealants were reclassified: 1-2 teeth = 3 UDA, 3+ teeth = 5 UDA.
