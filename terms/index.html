2. Description of Service

UDA Pulse is a personal productivity tool designed for dental professionals in the United Kingdom. It allows users to track Units of Dental Activity (UDAs), private income, CPD hours, lab bills, and open courses of treatment.

UDA Pulse is NOT a medical device. It is not intended to provide clinical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. It is solely a record-keeping and productivity tool.
